    The IPPC identifies phytosanitary information which is exchanged, by contracting parties, the Secretariat and at times the Regional Plant Protection Organizations (RPPOs). There are three categories of information that can be posted via the IPP in order to meet IPPC information exchange obligations: basic reporting (description of the NPPO, list of Entry points, lists of regulated pests, phytosanitary restrictions), event-driven reporting (non-compliance, emergency actions, pest report), and request-driven reporting (organizational arrangements, rationale for phytosanitary requirements, pest status).
